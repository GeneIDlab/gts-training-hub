Wednesday — in office

You’re in the field!

You’ll move into the office to begin hands-on work with your manager. Here’s what a screening day looks like once you start:

  • Prepare the daily list before clinic
  • If you have access, check the patient’s chart in the EMR for previous screening
  • Verify the patient has in-network insurance before screening
  • On BambooHR, clock in on arrival, take breaks, and clock out after UPS drop-off (UPS pick-up is scheduled with Ben)
  • Update the Microsoft Excel Test Log daily
  • Make sure the correct language is in the medical notes for each test type
  • Upload the insurance card and medical notes into GetGeneID
Always keep paper backups
Have 3 copies each of the paper requisition and checklist for all tests, in case of any issues with GetGeneID.
